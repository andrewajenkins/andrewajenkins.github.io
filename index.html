Clinical Trials Context Engine

The Problem

For the AI (TAP) to be useful, it couldn't just write generic code. It needed to understand the deep, complex, and highly specific domain of clinical trial data models (RTSM). A generic LLM has no concept of this specialized business logic.

My Solution

I built the "brain" for TAP. This involved creating a specialized RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) knowledge base from the ground up. I wrote scripts to ingest and process our application's entire set of Django models and existing test patterns. This gave the AI the specific "context" it needed to understand our business logic.

The Outcome

This Context Engine is what makes TAP truly intelligent. It enables the AI to generate code that is context-aware and specific to our clinical trials domain. It directly assists engineers by accelerating the difficult translation of complex business requirements into accurate test code.
